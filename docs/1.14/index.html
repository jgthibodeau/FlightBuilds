General:
  • Space - Flap wings
  • Q - Breath fire
On Ground:
  • WASD - Move
  • E - Attack
In Air:
  • W/S - L Wing up/down
  • A/D - L Wing in/out
  • Up/Down - R Wing up/down
  • Left/Right - R Wing in/out
General Flight:
  • Both wings neutral to glide.
  • Both wings up/down to control pitch
  • Alternate wings to roll
  • Both wings in to dive.
  • Both wings out to slow down and get more lift.
Flapping:
  • Flap with both wings neutral to gain upward speed.
  • Flap with both wings in to gain forward speed.
  • Flap with both wings out to gain backward speed.
