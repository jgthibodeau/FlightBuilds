General:
  • Space - Flap wings
  • Q - Breath fire
  • Arrows - Aim fire
  • Alt - Pick up items
  • C - Re-center camera
Ground:
  • WASD - Move
Flight:
  • W/S - Wings in/out - pitch
  • A/D - Roll
Flapping:
  • Space - Flap upward
  • Space + W - Flap forward
  • Space + S - Flap backward
Tips:
  • After running for a while, you will pick up speed
  • You can aim your flame breath in the air, and on the ground
  • Flap forward to gain speed, and backward to stop and hover
  • Flapping upward from the ground initially is the best way to take flight
