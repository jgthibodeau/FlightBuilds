General:
  • Space - Flap wings
  • Q - Breath fire
  • Arrows - Aim fire
  • E - Hold to pick up items
Ground:
  • WASD - Move
  • F - Attack
  • X - Air gust
Flight:
  • W/S - Wings in/out - pitch
  • A/D - Roll
  • X - Boost (hold to boost more)
Flapping:
  • Space - Flap upward
  • Space + W - Flap forward
  • Space + S - Stop and hover
Tips:
  • After running for a while, you will pick up speed
  • You can aim your flame breath in the air, and on the ground
  • Hold down the boost button to keep your wings in and go further
  • You can only boost so many times before you need to recharge your stamina (feather icons)
  • Continuous fire breathing will eventually decrease the size/distance of the flame. Wait some time for it to build back up (flame icon)
  • Flap backward to stop and hover
